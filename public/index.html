What is QR Me?

Imagine that you just met a new friend and wish to exchange contact info! But who actually wants to vocally spell out their phone number or email?

Instead, use QR Me! As you enter your info below, a QR code will be created with that info inside it.

Your new friend can then scan it on their phone, which will create a new contact for you!
